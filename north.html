Simple Roots Brewing

website
1127 North Ave #8
Burlington, VT 05408

Hours:
Wednesday-Thursday 4-9pm
Friday 3-9pm
Saturday 12-9pm
Sunday 12-5pm

Beer

  • Good draft list, ~10 or so
  • They use a pretty decent hop variety here, you'll find at least one you really like
  • Most of there beers you can get as a bomber
  • They will fill any clean growler as well, no badging required here

Food

  • They don't have any food themselves, but they have food trucks during certain days/times.
  • Check their website if you're interested

Notes

  • I like this place, and I believe this is a great example of how you make a marriage work, husband and wife start a brewery, live happily ever after
  • Good sized tasting room, simple and gets the job done

Four Quarters

website
14 minutes from Simple Roots
150 W Canal St
Winooski, VT 05404

Hours:
Friday 5-9pm
Saturday 12-9pm
Sunday 12-3pm

Beer

  • They do a good job of having a variety of beers at any time, usually ~ 10
  • They do a good job with some of their specialty brews, usually have a couple that have great unique flavors to them, case in point Great Cookie Bear
  • They do have can releases, but don't plan on having anything there to purchase besides growlers

Food

  • Hahaha, oh, ya.. no... no food

Notes

  • While larger than a whole in the wall, it's not a lot larger
  • Outside overflow is nice in the summer
  • Park at the place, don't bother trying to find parking on the Winooski circle, there's plenty of street parking closer
  • Been around a little over 3 years, I have this on the list cause this place is what you think of when it comes to small VT brewing

14th Star

website
35 Minute from Four Quarters
133 N Main St #7
St Albans City, VT 05478

Hours:(they do a weird split hour thing on some days as well, where they're open in the morning for a couple hours)
Tuesday-Friday 4-10pm
Saturday 11-10pm
Sunday 11-4pm
Closed Mondays

Beer

  • ~12 beers on draft at any point in time
  • They have some good specialty/rotation beers as well, so look for those
  • They can all their most popular beers, 16oz 4packs
  • They usually have a tap or 2 for guest beers as well

Food

  • No food there, at least the last time I went. But your kinda in St. Albans, so there's food around
  • And if you're feeling adventurous, there's a china wok buffet in the same building

Notes

  • It's north I know, but if you haven't been there, you should at least once
  • Good sized in-door tasting room
  • They have a ton of outdoor seating in the parking lot, so at least on nice day your whole bus-load can enjoy there
  • They're a verified venue on UnTappd so check our there active list there
