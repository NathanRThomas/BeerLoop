Burlington Beer

website
25 Omega Dr #150
Williston, VT 05495

Hours:
Monday-Thursday 12-7pm
Friday-Saturday 12-8pm
Sunday 12-5pm

Beer

  • One of my favorite things about this brewery is their rotating lines. You can essential be guaranteed a new beer you haven't had before each week
  • Usually pouring around 10 different beers on draft
  • They can almost everything, and the stuff they don't, they bottle
  • Super friendly staff will help you figure out what ages well, and what you need to drink right away
  • Flagship IIPA is the wizard, always pick up a 4pack when visiting
  • They also have two seasonal dark beers, Strange Apparition and Coniferous which, when aged, I believe are rivaled only by Hill Farmstead

Food

  • They have an ever growing list of food items including hot plates as well as a range of snacks
  • Good spot to have lunch

Notes

  • I know I keep saying this, but this is my favorite brewery
  • Just turned 3 years old and growing like crazy. Open 7 days a week now, with food, crazy
  • The overall space is tight, and it does get standing room only on busy nights and weekends
  • They do a good job with games. They have jenga and stuff, but they also have old-school nintendo games setup around, so enjoy

Goodwater Brewery

website
1 minute from Burlington Beer
740 Marshall Ave
Williston, VT 05495

Hours:
Wednesday-Friday 3-8pm
Saturday 12-8pm
Sunday 1-5pm

Beer

  • Strong english roots with this brewmaster's style
  • 4-6 different draft lines typically. They have occasional rotating speciality brews
  • They started canning almost right away, so most of their beers you can get, in either 12 or 16 oz cans.

Food

  • Not the last time i was there, no

Notes

  • They opened a little over a year ago, and talking with the brewmaster I had to agree with his style. Burlington doesn't need another double IPA It needs an english style mild bitter that people want as much as Heady Topper
  • Small tasting room, but typically not super busy, although don't get mad at me if they keep getting more popular

Stone Corral

website
15 Minute from Goodwater
83 Huntington Rd
Richmond, VT 05477

Hours:
Tuesday-Thursday 3-10pm
Friday-Saturday 12-10pm
Sunday 12-6pm
Closed Mondays

Beer

  • Full line of different styles of beer, you'll be able to find one you like here no matter your style
  • Always some seasonals/rotating specialty beers on draft as well
  • They do a good job with bombers here, you'll usually be able to take home some of your favorites

Food

  • This place is relatively new, and is growing all the time. As of now that have some pretty decent warm plates, burgers and stuff
  • Good for a quick bite

Notes

  • Kinda in the middle of no-where, but not that bad
  • Good sized tasting room and table space to eat
  • This is true almost everywhere, but I always find the bartenders super nice and knowledgeable here
